# Brand Design System: Dr. Grazelle (Proof & Practice)

This document outlines the visual identity for Dr. Grazelle's brand, "Proof & Practice," extracted from the live site and associated assets.

## 1. Brand Essence
- **Tone:** Professional yet approachable, clinical but human-centric, science-backed but practical.
- **Keywords:** Evidence-based, clarity, consistency, lifestyle medicine, longevity.

## 2. Color Palette
The brand uses a sophisticated, calming palette that balances clinical authority with wellness warmth.

### Primary Colors
- **Deep Navy (Background/Primary Text):** `#1A2B48` (approx) - Used for headers, footers, and primary body text for high contrast.
- **Steel Blue (Secondary Background):** `#5E7A9E` (approx) - Used for hero sections and call-outs.
- **Soft Slate:** `#F4F7FA` - Used for background sections to provide a clean, modern feel.

### Accent Colors
- **Golden Sand (CTA/Primary Accent):** `#E6C280` - Used for primary buttons and highlighting key phrases.
- **Vibrant Green (Iconography/Nature):** `#76C043` - Used for the seedling icon and wellness-related accents.
- **Pastel Tones (Categories):**
  - **Proof (Orange/Yellow):** `#F9D08B`
  - **Practice (Green/Teal):** `#A3D9C9`
  - **Purpose (Lavender/Purple):** `#C9BFE3`

## 3. Typography
The typography is clean and legible, using a mix of modern sans-serifs.

- **Headings:** Bold Sans-Serif (e.g., Inter, Montserrat, or similar system font).
  - Characterized by tight letter-spacing and high x-height.
  - Weight: Bold (700) or Extra Bold (800).
- **Body Copy:** Sans-Serif (e.g., Inter, Open Sans).
  - Clean, high-legibility font with generous line height (approx 1.6).
  - Color: Deep Navy or Dark Slate for readability.
- **Accents:** Small caps or italicized sans-serif for labels (e.g., category tags like "Proof," "Practice").

## 4. Visual Elements & Component Patterns

### Buttons (CTAs)
- **Primary:** Solid "Golden Sand" background, dark text, rounded corners (approx 8px).
- **Secondary:** Outlined buttons or text links with a chevron.

### Cards
- **Newsletter/Post Cards:** White background with subtle shadows or light gray borders.
- **Category Tags:** Rounded pill-shaped containers with pastel background colors corresponding to the category.

### Layout & Spacing
- **Grid:** Clean, spacious layout with significant vertical breathing room (whitespace).
- **Alignment:** Primarily left-aligned text for readability, with centered sections for high-impact statements.
- **Imagery:** High-quality, warm photography (often featuring Dr. Grazelle) and clean, technical diagrams (blueprints/schematics style).

### Iconography
- Hand-drawn or minimalist vector icons (e.g., the "seedling" leaf).
- Technical/Scientific diagrams using thin white or light blue lines on dark backgrounds.

## 5. UI Elements
- **Navigation:** Simple, top-aligned text links with a prominent "Subscribe" button.
- **Input Fields:** Large, white background fields with subtle borders and clear CTA integration.
- **Footer:** Deep navy background with white text, including social icons and a clear medical disclaimer.
