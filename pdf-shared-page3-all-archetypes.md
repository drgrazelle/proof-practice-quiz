**How to read the map**

Each archetype represents a real person trying to build sustainable health, and each one is dealing with a different gap. The further you sit from the center, the more pieces you're still building. The center is where the three pieces meet and the architecture holds itself up.

Most people are missing more than they realize. Most people also have more strength than they realize. The map shows you both at the same time.

The work is the same for everyone, even when the starting point is different: build the piece you're missing next.
